🛍 BIS Shopping Spree

Welcome to the **BIS Shopping Spree**, a fun and educational game where you learn about BIS-certified products!

🔍 What is BIS?

The **Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS)** ensures quality, safety, and reliability in consumer products. Look for the **BIS Certification Mark** when purchasing essential goods like helmets, electrical appliances, packaged food, and gold.

🎮 How to Play?

  • 🛒 Select **BIS-certified products** from the virtual store.
  • 🚫 Avoid counterfeit or unsafe products.
  • 💯 Earn points for choosing the right items.
  • ⏳ The game is **timed**. Pick as many correct items as possible before time runs out!
  • 🏆 The player with the highest score wins!
